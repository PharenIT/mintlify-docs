## 2\. Collection of Personal Data

### 2.1 Access Data

Whenever you access our website, we process technical connection data required for secure operation, content delivery and error analysis.

- browser type and version
- operating system
- referrer URL
- visited pages and files
- date and time of access
- anonymised IP address
- transferred data volume and provider

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.

Retention period: usually up to 30 days unless a longer period is required for security analysis.

## 3\. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to ensure website functionality, store preferences and analyse how our services are used.

- strictly necessary cookies for core features and security
- analytics cookies for anonymised reach measurement
- marketing cookies only with your explicit consent

Legal basis for consent-based cookies: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR.

You can change your choice at any time via the cookie banner or your browser settings.

## 4\. Tracking & Analytics Services

We only use external services where this is necessary for analytics, abuse prevention or marketing and the applicable data protection requirements are met.

Relevant providers and policies:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Meta Privacy Policy](https://www.facebook.com/privacy/explanation)
- [Automattic Privacy Policy](https://automattic.com/privacy/)
- [Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) - Opt-out plugin for Google Analytics

## 5\. Legal Bases

- Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR for consent
- Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR for pre-contractual measures and contract performance
- Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR for legal obligations
- Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR for legitimate interests, especially security, abuse prevention and product improvement

## 6\. Retention Period

We only retain personal data for as long as necessary for the relevant purpose or as long as statutory retention obligations apply. Data is then deleted or blocked.

## 7\. Your Rights

- access to the data stored about you
- rectification of inaccurate or incomplete data
- erasure or restriction of processing where legally permissible
- data portability
- objection to processing based on legitimate interests
- withdrawal of consent with effect for the future

Please send requests to info@pharen.de.

## 8\. Data Security

We implement technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against loss, manipulation and unauthorised access. These measures are reviewed and improved regularly.

Please note that no internet transmission can ever be completely secure despite appropriate safeguards.

## 9\. Disclosure to Third Parties

We only disclose personal data if you have consented, if disclosure is necessary for contract performance, if we are legally obliged to do so, or if we use external service providers under a data processing agreement.

For transfers to third countries, we ensure appropriate safeguards, in particular standard contractual clauses or comparable protection mechanisms.

## 10\. Contact Form & Email

### 10.1 Contact form

If you use our contact form, we process your name, email address, optionally your company and the content of your enquiry.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR or Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR where the enquiry relates to pre-contractual measures.

### 10.2 Email contact

If you contact us by email, we process the content you send exclusively to handle your enquiry and communicate with you.

### 10.3 Retention

Contact enquiries are only stored for as long as required to process and document the matter or as long as statutory duties require longer retention.
