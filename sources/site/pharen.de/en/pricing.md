# Current workspace plans

The prices on this page match the current values in the product. All workspace plans are billed monthly, while AI credits run separately as prepaid balance or an optional monthly subscription.

## Free

EUR 0

per workspace / month

For testing a single workspace with very low volume.

- 1 workspace member
- 1 team
- 10 workflow runs / month
- 100 list records total
- AI credits available separately

[Start for free](https://pharen.de/en/hub)

## Starter

EUR 20

per workspace / month

For small teams getting started with workflows, lists and AI.

- Up to 3 members
- 1 team
- 100 workflow runs / month
- 1,000 list records total
- Optional monthly AI credit subscription

[Start with Starter](https://pharen.de/en/hub)

## Team

EUR 50

per workspace / month

For growing teams with more users and more automation.

- Up to 5 members
- Up to 3 teams
- 500 workflow runs / month
- 5,000 list records total
- AI credits separately or as a monthly subscription

[Open in Hub](https://pharen.de/en/hub)

## Pro

Recommended

EUR 100

per workspace / month

For organisations with higher volume and more complex operations.

- Up to 50 members
- Up to 10 teams
- 5,000 workflow runs / month
- 50,000 list records total
- Built for advanced AI and workflow usage

[Use Pro in Hub](https://pharen.de/en/hub)

## Enterprise

Custom

Billing on request

For unlimited usage, special requirements and dedicated support.

- Unlimited members, teams and list records
- Unlimited workflow runs
- Flexible billing and AI credit models
- Own infrastructure or special setup possible
- Consulting at EUR 130 / hour

[Contact sales](https://pharen.de/en/contact)
