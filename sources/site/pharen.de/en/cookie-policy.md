# Cookie Policy

Information about the use of cookies and similar technologies on our website.

### Table of Contents

## 1\. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help store preferences, maintain sessions and evaluate usage patterns.

## 2\. Why do we use cookies?

- to ensure the technical functionality of our website
- to store preferences and consent choices
- to analyse reach and usage in an anonymised way
- to improve content and marketing activities

## 3\. Which cookies do we use?

### Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are required for core features and security mechanisms and cannot be disabled through our website.

Examples: consent status, session identifiers.

### Preference cookies

These cookies remember settings such as language or display preferences to make the site easier to use.

### Statistics cookies

Statistics cookies help us understand reach, page views and interactions in an anonymised manner so that we can improve our services.

Examples: Google Analytics or similar audience measurement.

### Marketing cookies

Marketing cookies help assess campaigns and make content more relevant. They are only set with your explicit consent.

## 4\. Managing cookie preferences

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time. Use our cookie banner, the relevant footer links or your browser settings to do so.

- open the cookie banner on first visit and adjust your choice
- reopen the settings later via the footer
- block or delete cookies at browser level

Please note that disabling certain cookies may limit website functionality.

## 5\. Further information

For more information about how we process personal data, please see our Privacy Policy. If you have questions about this Cookie Policy, please contact us by email.
