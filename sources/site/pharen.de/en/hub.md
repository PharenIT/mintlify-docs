## Enterprise-Grade Security

Configure the controls your organization actually needs: granular permissions, budget guardrails, approved AI models and providers, and stronger account security from day one.

### Granular Access and Governance

Define who can access which workspace areas, require stronger sign-in policies, and set clear guardrails for spend and AI usage across your teams.

- Custom roles and permissions
- 2FA and sign-in protection
- Audit-ready access history

### Custom Roles and Permissions

Control access by team, workspace area, and responsibility so each person sees only what they need.

### Custom Budget Controls

Set spending limits and approval rules for teams, workspaces, or AI-heavy processes before costs drift.

### Custom Model and Provider Policies

Choose which models and providers are allowed so teams work only with approved AI infrastructure.

### 2FA and Account Security

Protect logins with two-factor authentication and stronger access rules for internal and external users.
