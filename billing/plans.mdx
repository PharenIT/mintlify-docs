Pharen Hub bills per workspace, so every workspace you create can run on its own plan — mix and match to match the scale of each project. Whether you're a solo builder prototyping an idea or a large organization running production AI workflows, there's a plan sized for you. All plans are billed monthly with no long-term commitment required, and you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time.

## Which Plan Is Right for You?

### You should choose **Free** if…
You're a solo user who wants to explore Pharen Hub, prototype an automation, or evaluate the platform before purchasing. The free plan has no time limit — it stays free as long as you like.

### You should choose **Starter** if…
You work with one or two collaborators on a contained project and need a reliable monthly workflow allowance without a large spend. It's a great entry point for freelancers managing client work or small side projects.

### You should choose **Team** if…
Your work involves multiple distinct teams or workstreams within one workspace. The jump to three teams and 500 workflow runs per month is meaningful for growing companies running recurring automations.

### You should choose **Pro** if…
You're running production AI workflows, managing a department-sized team, or pushing the limits of automation. Pro is the recommended plan for organizations that want access to the full power of Pharen Hub without negotiating a custom contract.

### You should choose **Enterprise** if…
Your organization needs unlimited scale, wants to run Pharen Hub on its own infrastructure, requires a custom billing model, or has compliance requirements that standard plans don't accommodate. [Contact us](https://pharen.de/en/contact) to start a conversation.

<Tip>
  Not sure which plan to pick? Start on **Free** or **Starter** and upgrade as your usage grows — you'll never lose your data when changing plans.
</Tip>

## Upgrading and Downgrading

You can change your workspace plan at any time from **Workspace Settings → Billing**. Upgrades take effect immediately; the new plan's limits are available as soon as the payment is processed. When you downgrade, your plan changes at the end of the current billing period, giving you time to reduce usage if needed.

If a downgrade would put you over the lower plan's limits (for example, you have 8 members on Pro and want to move to Team), Pharen Hub will prompt you to resolve those conflicts before the change is confirmed.
